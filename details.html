Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet and closest to the Sun. It has extreme temperatures and no atmosphere to protect it.

Venus

Venus is covered in thick clouds of acid and is hotter than any other planet. Often called Earth’s “sister planet.”

Earth

Earth is the only known planet with life, liquid water, and a breathable atmosphere. Our home world.

Mars

Mars is known as the Red Planet due to its iron-rich soil. Scientists believe it once had oceans.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system with a massive storm called the Great Red Spot.

Saturn

Saturn is famous for its beautiful rings made of ice and rock. It has over 80 moons.

Uranus

Uranus is a cold, icy giant that rotates on its side, making its seasons extremely unusual.

Neptune

Neptune is the farthest planet from the Sun, known for its deep blue color and strong winds.

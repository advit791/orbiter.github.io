About the Creator

Orbiter was created by Advit(Advit791) and Shaurya(SPS), two developers who blend robotics, coding, and futuristic UI design into one universe.

The goal of Orbiter is simple: make space feel alive. Bring the cosmos to your screen.

This project is constantly evolving — new features, new ideas, and new experiments are always in orbit.
